Live wedding and event watercolour painting in Melbourne and Sydney

I am Chris Wang, a watercolour artist based in Melbourne. I paint live at weddings, corporate events, brand activations and private celebrations: a hand-painted watercolour keepsake for each of your guests, painted while the night carries on, and a portrait of the two of you painted first while everything is fresh.

The base package is from $1000: three hours of live painting, around 24 keepsakes on 300gsm A5 cotton paper, every piece sleeved to take home on the night. Travel is included within about an hour of Melbourne or Sydney metro, and I travel Australia-wide for weddings and events further afield.

Studio commissions from your photographs are also available, including A3 family and bridal-party portraits from $400 and wedding bouquet illustrations from $500.
