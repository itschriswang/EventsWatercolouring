Drawn first,
then painted

I trained as an architect, so every portrait starts as a drawing. On your wedding day I build that drawing in the room and let the watercolour arrive, live, while the celebration is still happening.

Scale 1:1 · painted by hand

How a live day runs

02 / Process
01

Enquire and hold the date

You send your date and venue, I confirm what I have open, and a deposit holds the day. I take a limited number of weddings each season.

02

Plan the set-up

We agree on hours, the guest pieces, and where I work, usually a quiet corner of the reception with a clear view of the room.

03

Paint live through the day

I draw and paint guests as small keepsakes and build the couple portrait as the evening moves. Watching it come together tends to become a highlight.

04

Keepsakes home, portrait follows

Guests take their sleeved pieces on the night. The framed couple portrait and high-resolution scans follow once any final refining is done in the studio.
