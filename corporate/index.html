Live watercolour painting for corporate events and brand activations

I am Christopher Wang, a watercolour artist based in Melbourne. I paint guests live at brand activations, product launches, client and VIP dinners, end of year parties, conferences and private functions across Melbourne, Sydney and Australia-wide.

A painter working live draws a crowd and gives your event a centre of gravity. Each piece takes five to ten minutes, around 8 an hour, and every guest leaves holding a hand-painted keepsake that gets kept and framed, with your event as the story behind it.

The base engagement is from $1000: three hours of live painting, around 24 keepsakes on 300gsm A5 cotton paper, every piece sleeved on the spot. Extra hours, multi-day activations and an after-event studio service are available. Public liability insurance included.
