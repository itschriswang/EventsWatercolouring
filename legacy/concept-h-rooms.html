03 / Approach

I trained as an architect, then went back to painting people. The discipline is mine. The bloom is the paint's.

COMPOSITION FIRST I build a portrait the way I once built a drawing, with structure underneath and restraint everywhere else.

ON THE NIGHT Working live changes the room. Guests gather to watch a face appear out of a wash, and the painting carries the feeling of the evening.
