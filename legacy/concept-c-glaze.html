Live wedding watercolour · Sydney

Layered by hand, lifted into light

I paint your wedding live, building each portrait the way watercolour wants to be built, one luminous wash over another.

Scroll

A watercolour is only ever as good as its restraint. I work wash over wash, letting each layer dry into the next, so the colour glows rather than sits flat.

I trained as an architect and came back to painting, and I carry that discipline into a loose, living medium. On the day I work quietly at the edge of the room, painting guests as small keepsakes and building the couple portrait as the light changes.

Everything is made by hand on archival cotton-rag paper. Each guest piece is sleeved to take home, and you receive high-resolution scans of every finished work.
