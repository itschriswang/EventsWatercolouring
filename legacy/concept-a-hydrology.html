Live wedding watercolour · Sydney

Painted while it happens

I set up in the room and paint your wedding as it unfolds. Guests leave with a small portrait in hand, and you keep a framed piece that holds the feeling of the night.

Scroll to see the work

I trained as an architect before I came back to painting. I bring that same eye to a wedding, composition first, restraint always, and a warmth that holds the whole room.

Watercolour rewards speed and nerve, which suits a live day. I work quickly and quietly at the edge of the celebration, reading faces and light, and the painting becomes part of the evening rather than a service tucked away in a corner.

Everything is made by hand on archival cotton-rag paper. Each guest piece is sleeved to take home, and you receive high-resolution scans of every finished work.

The work

Live pieces from the day and studio commissions from photographs. Replace with final portfolio scans and captions.
